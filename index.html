PanelPulse

Interactive visualizations demonstrating the relationship between dust accumulation and solar panel efficiency, supporting research on "Energy and Cost Efficient Self-Cleaning Solar Panels."

Dust vs Efficiency Correlation

This visualization shows how dust accumulation directly impacts solar panel efficiency, highlighting the inverse relationship between dust levels and energy production.

Time Series Analysis

Track how dust accumulation and panel efficiency change over time, helping identify patterns and optimal cleaning intervals.
